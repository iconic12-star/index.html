Antarjaal (Internet) [Sampaadit karein]

Vishwa ka sabse bada sanchar tantra

Sankhipt Jaankari
🌐
Sthapana1983
PrakarVaishvik Neta
Upayogkarta5+ Billion
ProtocolTCP/IP
NirmataARPANET

Antarjaal ya Internet ek vaishvik sanchar tantra hai jo lakho computer networks ko aapas mein jodata hai. Yeh TCP/IP protocol ka upayog karke data ko ek jagah se doosri jagah bhejta hai. Aaj ke samay mein poori duniya mein 5 billion se adhik log internet ka upayog karte hain.

Internet ne logon ke jeevan ko bahut prabhavit kiya hai — shiksha, vyaapar, manoranjan aur sanchar — sabhi kshetron mein krantikari parivartan aaya hai.

1. Itihaas [Sampaadit karein]

Mukhya lekh: ARPANET ka Itihaas

Internet ki neev 1960 ke dashak mein ARPANET project ke roop mein rakhi gayi thi. Yeh project United States Department of Defense ne shuru kiya tha. Pehle sirf kuch universities aur sarkari sansthayein isse use karti theen.

1991 mein Tim Berners-Lee ne World Wide Web (WWW) ka aavishkar kiya, jisne internet ko aam logon ke liye uplabdh karaya. Iske baad internet ne tezi se poori duniya mein apni pahnuch banayi.

"The Web is more a social creation than a technical one."
— Tim Berners-Lee

2. Kaise Kaam Karta Hai [Sampaadit karein]

Internet lakhon computers aur devices ko ek doosre se optical fiber cables, wireless signals aur satellites ke zariye jodata hai.

2.1 TCP/IP Protocol

Data ko chote-chote packets mein divide karke bheja jaata hai. Har packet apna rasta khud dhundhta hai aur destination par pahunchne ke baad phir se jod diya jaata hai.

2.2 Domain Name System (DNS)

DNS ek phone book ki tarah kaam karta hai. Jab aap google.com likhte hain, DNS usse ek IP address mein badal deta hai jaise 142.250.182.46.

3. Upayog [Sampaadit karein]

Internet ke pramukh upayog:

  • Sanchar — Email, WhatsApp, Video Call
  • Shiksha — Online courses, YouTube, Wikipedia
  • Vyaapar — E-commerce, Banking, Digital payments
  • Manoranjan — OTT platforms, Online games, Social media
  • Sarkar — Digital India, e-governance, online forms

4. Bharat mein Internet [Sampaadit karein]

Bharat mein internet ki shuruat 15 August 1995 ko VSNL dwara ki gayi thi. 2016 mein Jio ke aane ke baad internet ki rasai aam logon tak pahunchi aur data ki kimatein bahut kam ho gayin.

Aaj Bharat duniya mein sabse saste internet data wale deshon mein se ek hai. Bharat mein lagbhag 75 crore internet upayogkarta hain.
